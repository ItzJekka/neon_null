ABOUT THE GAME

Embark on an interstellar roguelike adventure where geometry meets galactic warfare. Navigate through procedurally generated cosmic landscapes filled with hostile geometric entities: squares, diamonds, and circles, each with unique attack patterns and behaviors.

Every run is different. Every death teaches. Every victory feels earned. Collect cosmic fragments, unlock new abilities, and transform your ship into the ultimate shape-shifting weapon.

◆ PROCEDURAL CHAOS

Infinite galaxies with unique geometric patterns and enemy configurations every playthrough.

■ SHAPE MASTERY

Battle and absorb the powers of squares, diamonds, and circles to evolve your abilities.

● COSMIC PROGRESSION

Permanent upgrades and unlockable ship modifications that persist between runs.
